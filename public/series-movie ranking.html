The Best Series/Movie According to Hena

Series which are my favourite.

Series:

1. Happiness

It's a kdrama with a great fast-paced storyline, character-centric approach to the zombie genre and strong performancees from leads Han Hyo-Joo and Park Hyung-Sik.
One of the main reasons that held me engaged while watching this.

2.The Penthouse

It's a high stakes South Korean drama with lots of suspense, mystery involving murder, revenge, crimes, secrets etc.
Someone who loves watching this kind of stuff, then it's definitely worth watching.

Movies which are my favourite.

Movies:

Sanam Teri Kasam

It is a romantic tradegy movie about a simple librarian, Saraswati (Saru), and a brooding ex-convict, Inder, who fall in love despite societal judgment and family rejection.
It is a story of longing and loss where a transformative romance between two mismatched people ends in tradegy due to terminal illness.

3 Idiots

It tells the story of three friends, Rancho, Farhan and Raju, at an elite engineering college, focusing on themes of friendships, pursuing passion over success, and creative learning.
It's a lighthearted movie with a great message; it maintains a balance throughout by incorporating both humor and numerous hidden messages.
For those who feel lost or unsure about their career choices, I believe this is one of the best movies to watch.
